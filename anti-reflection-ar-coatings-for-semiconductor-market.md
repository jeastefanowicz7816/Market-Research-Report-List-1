<p><p>Anti-Reflection (AR) coatings in the semiconductor market enhance light transmission and reduce reflections on surfaces. Bottom anti-reflective coatings (BARC) are applied beneath photoresists to improve resolution during lithography. Top anti-reflective coatings (TARC) are applied on the surface to minimize light reflections during exposure. Silicon anti-reflective coatings utilize silicon-based materials to enhance performance in photonic applications, while carbon coatings offer excellent optical properties and durability. Each type serves specific functions in optimizing semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
