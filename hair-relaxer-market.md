<p><strong>The Hair Relaxer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lye Relaxer</li><li>No Lye Relaxer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hair relaxer market is primarily divided into two categories: lye relaxers and no-lye relaxers. Lye relaxers contain sodium hydroxide, offering faster results and more effective straightening but can be harsh on the scalp. On the other hand, no-lye relaxers utilize calcium hydroxide and are generally milder, making them suitable for sensitive skin. While no-lye relaxers may require additional maintenance to achieve desired results, both types address different hair textures and personal preferences in the quest for smoother, straighter hair.</p></p>
