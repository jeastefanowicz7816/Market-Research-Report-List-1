<p><strong>The Special Mortar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Special Mortar</li><li>Wet Mixed Special Mortar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Special Mortar market is divided into two main types: Dry Special Mortar and Wet Mixed Special Mortar. Dry Special Mortar is a pre-mixed, powdered formulation that requires water to activate, making it easy to transport and store. It typically offers longer shelf life and flexibility in mixing ratios. In contrast, Wet Mixed Special Mortar is delivered in a ready-to-use state, requiring no additional water. This type allows for immediate application, providing convenience on construction sites and ensuring consistent performance.</p></p>
