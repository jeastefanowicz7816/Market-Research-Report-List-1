<p><p>Epitaxy equipment markets primarily feature two key types: Metal-Organic Chemical Vapor Deposition (MOCVD) and High-Temperature Chemical Vapor Deposition (HT CVD). MOCVD is utilized for producing compound semiconductors, especially in optoelectronic applications like LEDs and laser diodes, by allowing precise control over material composition. HT CVD, on the other hand, operates at elevated temperatures to grow high-quality thin films for various semiconductor applications. Both technologies are essential for advancing microelectronics, photovoltaics, and emerging technologies in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
