<p><p>The car audio amplifiers market is divided into OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) and Aftermarket segments. OEM amplifiers are factory-installed by vehicle manufacturers, offering basic audio performance tailored to standard systems. They are integrated seamlessly into the car's design but may lack advanced features. Aftermarket amplifiers are purchased separately and installed post-purchase, catering to enthusiasts seeking higher power, better sound quality, and customization. They offer flexibility, superior performance, and compatibility with upgraded audio systems, appealing to users wanting enhanced audio experiences beyond factory standards.</p></p>
