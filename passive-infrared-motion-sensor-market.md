<p><p>The Passive Infrared Motion Sensor market includes various types such as accelerometers, gyroscopes, magnetometers, and combo sensors. Accelerometers detect changes in motion and orientation, enabling devices to sense movement. Gyroscopes measure rotational motion, enhancing stability and tracking. Magnetometers sense magnetic fields, providing location data and orientation. Combo sensors integrate multiple functionalities, combining the capabilities of the other types for more accurate motion detection and enhanced performance across applications like security, automation, and gaming.</p></p>
