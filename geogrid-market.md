<p><strong>The Geogrid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Uniaxial</li><li>Biaxial</li><li>Triaxial</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The geogrid market is categorized into uniaxial, biaxial, and triaxial types, each serving distinct applications. Uniaxial geogrids are primarily used for reinforcement in slopes and retaining walls, as they provide strength in one direction. Biaxial geogrids offer stability in two dimensions, making them suitable for base course reinforcement in roads and pavements. Triaxial geogrids, designed with a three-dimensional structure, enhance load distribution and are effective in soil stabilization and earth structures, adapting to complex load conditions.</p></p>
