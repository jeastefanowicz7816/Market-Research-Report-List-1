<p><p>The semiconductor industry relies on specialized pumps for vacuum and pressure control. **Dry pumps** are oil-free, offering clean, low-maintenance operation, ideal for contamination-sensitive processes. **Molecular pumps** use high-speed rotors to achieve ultra-high vacuum levels, essential for precise semiconductor manufacturing. **Other pumps** include diaphragm, scroll, and turbomolecular pumps, each tailored for specific applications like etching, deposition, or ion implantation. These pumps ensure efficient, contamination-free environments, critical for producing high-quality semiconductors with nanometer-scale precision.</p></p>
