<p><p>Solar Water Heaters (SWH) are devices that utilize solar energy to heat water for residential or commercial use. The market can be divided into two main types: compact type and split type. Compact type systems combine solar collectors and storage tanks in a single unit, making them space-efficient and ideal for smaller installations. Split type systems feature separate collectors and storage tanks, allowing for greater flexibility in installation and often higher efficiency, making them suitable for larger properties or diverse climates.</p></p>
