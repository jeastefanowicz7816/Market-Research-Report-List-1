<p><p>The polymeric membrane market for separation includes various types such as hollow fiber, spiral wound, and others. Hollow fiber membranes consist of thin, tube-like structures with high surface area, ideal for applications like water purification and gas separation. Spiral wound membranes are layered sheets rolled into a compact form, commonly used in desalination and wastewater treatment. Other types include flat sheet and tubular membranes, catering to diverse industrial needs like food processing and chemical separation. Each type offers unique advantages in efficiency, scalability, and application-specific performance.</p></p>
