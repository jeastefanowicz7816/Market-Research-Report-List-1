<p><p>The high voltage divider market includes two main types: capacitive voltage dividers and compensated resistive voltage dividers. Capacitive voltage dividers use capacitors to reduce high voltages for accurate measurement in electrical systems. They offer high input impedance and frequency response. Compensated resistive voltage dividers utilize resistors with careful design to minimize load effects and maintain accuracy across varying voltages. Both types are crucial for ensuring safe and precise voltage monitoring in various industrial and laboratory applications.</p></p>
