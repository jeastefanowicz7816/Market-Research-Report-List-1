<p><p>The single-use centrifuge machines market comprises two primary types: benchtop centrifuges and continuous flow centrifuges. Benchtop centrifuges are compact, designed for small-scale processing and often used in laboratories for quick sample separations. In contrast, continuous flow centrifuges are optimized for larger-scale operations, allowing for the uninterrupted processing of fluids, making them suitable for industrial applications. Both types leverage single-use technology to minimize contamination risks and reduce cleaning requirements, enhancing operational efficiency.</p></p>
