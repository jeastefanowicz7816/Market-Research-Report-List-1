<p><p>The carbon steel tubes and pipes market is primarily divided into two types: welded and seamless. Welded tubes and pipes are produced by joining steel strips or plates through welding, making them suitable for low-pressure applications. Meanwhile, seamless tubes and pipes are manufactured from solid round steel and are ideal for high-pressure environments, offering greater strength and durability. Both types serve various industries, including construction, automotive, and oil and gas, catering to specific needs based on their manufacturing processes.</p></p>
