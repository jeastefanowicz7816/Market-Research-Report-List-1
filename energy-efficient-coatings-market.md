<p><p>Energy-efficient coatings enhance thermal performance, reducing energy consumption. **Phase Change Thermal Insulation Coatings** absorb and release heat during phase transitions, maintaining stable temperatures. **Vacuum Thermal Insulation Coatings** use vacuum-sealed layers to minimize heat transfer, offering superior insulation. **Reflective Thermal Insulation Coatings** reflect sunlight and infrared radiation, reducing heat absorption. **Other** types include hybrid or multifunctional coatings combining insulation, durability, and aesthetic properties. These coatings are widely used in construction, automotive, and industrial applications to improve energy efficiency, reduce costs, and support sustainability.</p></p>
