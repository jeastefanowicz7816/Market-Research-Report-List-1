<p><p>The solvent recovery equipment market is divided into two main types: on-site and off-site recovery equipment. On-site solvent recovery equipment is installed at the location where solvents are used, allowing businesses to directly recycle solvents, reducing waste and costs. Off-site solvent recovery equipment involves transporting used solvents to a designated facility for processing and recovery. This method is often utilized by companies that can't accommodate large systems on their premises, ensuring compliance with environmental regulations while maximizing solvent reuse.</p></p>
