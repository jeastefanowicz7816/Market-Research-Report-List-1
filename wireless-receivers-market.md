<p><ul><li>Inductive Coupling Wireless Power Receivers</li><li>Capacitive Coupling Wireless Power Receivers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless receivers market is primarily categorized into inductive coupling and capacitive coupling wireless power receivers. Inductive coupling uses magnetic fields to transfer energy between coils, making it ideal for applications like charging electric vehicles and smartphones. Capacitive coupling, on the other hand, utilizes electric fields for energy transfer, suitable for low-power devices such as wearables and IoT gadgets. Both types offer unique benefits and cater to various applications, driving innovation in wireless power technologies.</p></p>
