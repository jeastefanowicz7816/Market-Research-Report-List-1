<p><p>The Home Meal Replacement (HMR) market includes three main types: Ready to Cook (RTC), Ready to Heat (RTH), and Ready to Eat (RTE). RTC products require preparation before consumption, allowing for customization while still saving time. RTH options are pre-cooked meals that need brief heating before serving, offering convenience with minimal effort. RTE meals are fully prepared and can be consumed immediately, catering to consumers seeking the ultimate convenience in meal solutions. Each type addresses varying consumer needs for time and preparation.</p></p>
