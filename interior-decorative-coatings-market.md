<p><strong>The Interior Decorative Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Waterborne</li><li>Solvent-borne</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The interior decorative coatings market is primarily categorized into two types: waterborne and solvent-borne coatings. Waterborne coatings utilize water as the primary solvent, offering low VOC emissions, quick drying times, and ease of cleanup, making them environmentally friendly. In contrast, solvent-borne coatings contain organic solvents, providing durability and superior adhesion on various surfaces. While solvent-borne options often offer a smoother finish and greater resistance to wear, their VOC content raises environmental and health concerns, influencing market preferences.</p></p>
