<p><p>Laboratory safety cabinets are crucial for protecting users and the environment from potential biological hazards. Class I Biological Safety Cabinets provide basic protection by drawing air through a HEPA filter, ensuring safe ventilation. Class II Biological Safety Cabinets offer enhanced protection, featuring laminar airflow and multiple compartments for safer handling of pathogens. Class III Biological Safety Cabinets provide maximum containment, with airtight construction and glove ports for manipulations, ideal for high-risk pathogens and rigorous laboratory work.</p></p>
