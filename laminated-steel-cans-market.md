<p><p>The laminated steel cans market primarily consists of two types: 2-piece cans and 3-piece cans. </p><p>2-piece cans are made from a single sheet of metal, formed into a cylindrical shape with a base, offering enhanced strength and reduced material usage. In contrast, 3-piece cans are constructed from three separate components: two ends and a cylindrical body, typically welded or soldered together. Each type caters to different packaging needs, with 2-piece cans being preferred for lighter products, while 3-piece cans provide versatility for various contents.</p></p>
