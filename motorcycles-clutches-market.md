<p><p>The motorcycle clutch market includes manual and semi-automatic types. Manual clutches require the rider to engage and disengage the clutch lever manually, offering full control over gear shifts, ideal for performance and customization. Semi-automatic clutches combine manual gear shifting with automatic clutch operation, eliminating the need for a clutch lever, making them user-friendly, especially for beginners or urban riders. Both types cater to different riding preferences, balancing control and convenience in various motorcycle applications.</p></p>
