<p><strong>The Radiographic Testing Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Volumetric Examination</li><li>Surface Examination</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiographic testing equipment market is divided into two main types: volumetric examination and surface examination. Volumetric examination focuses on assessing internal structures and defects within materials using techniques like X-ray and gamma-ray imaging, ideal for thick or dense components. Surface examination, on the other hand, evaluates external characteristics and defects, utilizing methods such as ultrasonic testing and eddy current testing. Each type serves distinct applications in industries like manufacturing, construction, and aerospace, ensuring quality and safety in various materials.</p></p>
