<p><p>The battery separator paper market consists of two main types: dry separator paper and wet separator paper. Dry separator paper is characterized by its ability to maintain high porosity and low moisture absorption, enhancing overall battery performance and lifespan. Wet separator paper, on the other hand, is designed for applications requiring higher electrolyte retention, improving ionic conductivity. Both types are crucial for different battery technologies, influencing energy density, safety, and efficiency in applications such as electric vehicles and consumer electronics.</p></p>
