<p><ul><li>Single-stage Torque Converter</li><li>Multistage Torque Converter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Transmission Torque Converter market includes two main types: Single-stage and Multistage Torque Converters. Single-stage torque converters are simpler, cost-effective, and commonly used in standard passenger vehicles, offering smooth power transfer and basic functionality. Multistage torque converters, with multiple turbine and stator sets, provide enhanced efficiency, better torque multiplication, and improved performance, making them suitable for heavy-duty vehicles and high-performance applications. Both types facilitate seamless power transmission between the engine and transmission, ensuring optimal vehicle operation.</p></p>
