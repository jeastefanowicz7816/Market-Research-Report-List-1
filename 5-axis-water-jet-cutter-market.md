<p><p>The 5-axis water-jet cutter market comprises two main types: pure water-jet cutters and abrasive water-jet cutters. Pure water-jet cutters utilize high-pressure water streams to cut softer materials like foam, rubber, or food products, ensuring clean and precise cuts without introducing contaminants. In contrast, abrasive water-jet cutters incorporate granular materials like garnet to enhance cutting capabilities, enabling them to slice through harder materials such as metals and ceramics. Both technologies offer versatility and precision for various industrial applications.</p></p>
