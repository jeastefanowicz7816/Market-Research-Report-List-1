<p><ul><li>Vertical Cyclic Corrosion Testers</li><li>Horizontal Cyclic Corrosion Testers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cyclic corrosion testers market is segmented into vertical and horizontal testers, each designed to simulate environmental exposure conditions for materials and coatings. Vertical cyclic corrosion testers position samples upright, facilitating runoff and mimicking natural rain effects, which is ideal for automotive and aerospace applications. In contrast, horizontal cyclic corrosion testers mount samples flat, allowing for uniform spray and condensation exposure, suitable for marine and industrial components. Both types aim to assess durability and corrosion resistance in an accelerated manner.</p></p>
