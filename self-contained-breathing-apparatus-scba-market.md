<p><strong>The Self Contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBA) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open-Circuit SCBA</li><li>Closed-Circuit SCBA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Self Contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBA) systems are vital for providing breathable air in hazardous environments. The market is primarily divided into two types: Open-Circuit SCBA and Closed-Circuit SCBA. Open-Circuit SCBA expels exhaled air into the atmosphere, making it suitable for firefighting and industrial applications. In contrast, Closed-Circuit SCBA, also known as rebreathers, recirculate exhaled air after removing carbon dioxide, offering longer durations and efficiency, ideal for underwater diving or confined spaces. Both systems cater to specific safety needs in various industries.</p></p>
