<p><p>The railway fasteners market comprises two main types: bolt-type and non-bolt-type fasteners. Bolt-type fasteners, including track bolts and rail anchors, provide secure connections for rail tracks and components, ensuring stability and safety. Non-bolt-type fasteners, such as clips and elastic fasteners, offer alternative methods of securing tracks, facilitating easier maintenance and installation. These fasteners are crucial for effective railway infrastructure, contributing to operational efficiency, reducing wear, and enhancing overall performance in rail networks.</p></p>
