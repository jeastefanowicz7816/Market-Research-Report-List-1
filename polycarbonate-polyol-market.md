<p><p>The polycarbonate polyol market is primarily divided into two types: liquid and solid polycarbonate polyols. Liquid polycarbonate polyols are viscous materials used in applications such as coatings, adhesives, and flexible foams due to their excellent flexibility and durability. In contrast, solid polycarbonate polyols offer higher rigidity and mechanical strength, making them suitable for applications like rigid foams and high-performance adhesives. Both types are valued for their thermal stability and resistance to chemicals, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
