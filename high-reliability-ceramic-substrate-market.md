<p><ul><li>HTCC Substrate</li><li>LTCC Substrate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-reliability ceramic substrate market is primarily divided into two types: HTCC (High-Temperature Cofired Ceramic) substrates and LTCC (Low-Temperature Cofired Ceramic) substrates. HTCC substrates are designed to withstand elevated temperatures and are often used in military and aerospace applications due to their superior thermal and mechanical properties. In contrast, LTCC substrates enable the integration of passive components during the co-firing process at lower temperatures, making them ideal for compact electronic devices and telecommunications applications.</p></p>
