<p><strong>The Data Center Liquid Cooling System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Indirect Liquid Cooling System</li><li>Direct Liquid Cooling System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Data Center Liquid Cooling System market comprises two primary types: Indirect Liquid Cooling Systems and Direct Liquid Cooling Systems. Indirect Liquid Cooling Systems utilize a heat exchanger to transfer heat away from equipment to a secondary cooling medium, minimizing direct contact with sensitive components. In contrast, Direct Liquid Cooling Systems involve liquid flowing directly over the components, providing efficient heat removal. Both systems aim to enhance thermal management, improve energy efficiency, and reduce reliance on traditional air cooling methods in data centers.</p></p>
